How can I add Cities?

There are multiple ways to add a city.

  • By allowing access to your location, via the initial popup
  • By long pressing anywhere on the map, if there is a city close by your press than this will be added to your locations
  • Via the cities menu, tap on the top left menu button in home screen. Here you can search for through many cities all over the world.

How can I remove a location

You can remove a location via the cities menu which can be opened via the top left menu button. In this menu you can remove a city bu:

  • Swipping the cell to the left
  • Tapping the edit button on the bottom left corner, you can than tap the `delete icon` on any cell and confirm it by tapping `delete button` that appeared on the right
    • It is also possible to delete all cities at once by tapping on the `edit` button on the bottom left and than tapping on the `delete all` button. You will be asked to confirm this action.

    How can I close a side menu

    There are multiple options for this

    • You can close a side menu by dragging it to the screen edge
    • By tapping the close or cancel button
    • On phones by tapping anywhere outside the side menu
